Welcome to GeoPlatform.

Geo-Platform is geoSDI framework for the creation and the development of geoportals. Geo-Platform five pillars are :

Simplicity | Modularity | Flexibility, through widgets | Scalability | Power

Geo-Platform offers skills on a wide basis, to be expanded according to specific requirements, taking advantage of the development of tailor-made widgets. Its components are two big modules, which make it scalable and powerful:

Geo-Platform Services allow the business logic to be separated from the userâ€™s interface. As far as concerns the cluster, Geo-platform can be scaled according to several needs. An API client allows the connection with the interface.

Geo-Platform GUI is the structure taking care of whatever concerns the interface and the stack of services. GUI section contains several under-modules offering functions on different levels.
